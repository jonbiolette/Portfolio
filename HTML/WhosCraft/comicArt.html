The Three Amigo's

These three always look like they haven’t eaten for the entire week. Even though pets don’t know our language, at some moments, you do feel connected with them. How amazing, especially when you have food in your hands!

We have three Russian tortoises all cohabitating together in a large enclosure we hand made last fall. Fred was our first, she was a resue that we recieved from a friend of ours that could no longer care for her. Major was next he was purchased at a pet store when we were just looking for hides for Fred, but when we saw him we had to get him! And Noko our baby, was also a rescue. Even though they are small and move slow you can always tell who is who based on their distict personalities!
